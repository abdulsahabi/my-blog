Who Am I? (Hint: Not a Superhero, Just Sahabi Abdulrahaman)
Welcome to my official personal webpage. Yes, I am Sahabi Abdulrahaman. Please pardon me for mentioning it again, but feel like insisted on it. :)
A Little More About Me:

I studied computer science at the prestigious Waziri Umaru Federal Polytechnic, Birnin Kebbi (WUFPBK), Kebbi state, Nigeria. Since I was young, I’ve been passionate about becoming a software engineer and a machine learning (ML) researcher. My love for computers started when I realized they could solve problems faster than I could finish a cooking rice (which, trust me, is fast).

My Academic Journey:

Studying at WUFPBK was one of my dreams, even though by the time I gained admission, they hadn't yet offered a software engineer program. I’m thrilled to have achieved it by completing my National Diploma (ND) in computer science in just two years, from 2021 to 2023. It was a rollercoaster ride of theory, coding, problem solving, and challenges, but I made it through! it's all memories now. Alhamdu lillaah.
